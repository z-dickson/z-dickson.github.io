Teaching

My teaching experience includes both substantive political science coursework as well as quantitative and computational methods and research design. I have taught courses at the undergraduate and graduate levels, and have experience teaching in-person and online. During my PhD, I taught courses at the University of Strathclyde and at the University of Glasgow. Some of the courses I taught include:

Politics

  • Comparative Politics (Undergraduate - University of Glasgow)
  • Introduction to Politics (Undergraduate - University of Glasgow)
  • Politics: Decision Making & Outcomes (Undergraduate - University of Strathclyde)

Quantitative Methods

  • Quantitative Methods 1: Statistics & Analysis (Undergraduate - University of Strathclyde)
  • Quantitative Methods 2: Statistics & Analysis (Undergraduate - University of Strathclyde)
  • Machine Learning for Data Analytics (Graduate - University of Strathclyde)
